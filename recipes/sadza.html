Sadza Recipe

How to prepare Sadza

Sadza or Pap is a popular dish in Southern Africa which is made from maize meal. It can be eaten with a number of relish, for example, chicken, beef, pork, etc.

You need 200grams of mealie-meal and water, you need to boil the water and on the side take some mealie-meal and mix it with cold water to create a paste, which you ten pour into the boiling water, stir until well mixed and allow to boil for 5 minutes. After which you then add more mealie-meal and stir until its firm. Stirring is done to mix it well and also to prevent lumps.

After that simmer for two minutes and then serve it with beef stew, or chicken stew, or offals, etc.

Ingredients

  • mealie-meal
  • water

Steps

  1. put 3 cups in a kettle to boil.
  2. in a bowl add half cup of mealie-meal and cold water and mix to form a paste.
  3. once the water boils mix the paste and the boiling water is a pot and stir until well mixed like porridge.
  4. allow it to boil for 5-10 minutes.
  5. add more mealie-meal and stir until its firm.
  6. simmer for 2 minutes and then serve the sadza.
