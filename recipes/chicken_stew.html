Chicken Stew Recipe

How to prepare Chicken Stew

We have two kinds of chicken, the one that cooks for a short period of time, and the road runners that you have to cook for a long period of time. If you are cooking a roadrunner you have to double the boiling time, sometimes up to 30 minutes. For regular chicken you can boil it for 15 minutes, then add some oil and fry it for a few minutes, you can add tomatoes and onions for soup.

Ingredients

  • chicken cuts
  • one tomato
  • half onion
  • cooking oil
  • salt
  • water

Steps

  1. put your chicken cuts in a pot and pour some water to boil.
  2. allow to boil for 15-30 minutes.
  3. cut the tomato and onions.
  4. after 15-30 minutes, drain the stork and add oil.
  5. fry for 10-15 minutes.
  6. then add the onions and tomatoes.
  7. let it cook for 5 minutes then add the stork.
  8. add some salt and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
