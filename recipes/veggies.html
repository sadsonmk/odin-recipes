Veggies Recipe

How to prepare the green veggies

Vegetables are an important part of our diet, and in Zimbabwe we have these as part of our main meals.

Ingredients

  • green vegetables(cabbage, lettuce, brocolli, cauliflower, etc)
  • salt
  • cooking oil

Steps

  1. cut you veggies
  2. heat some oil
  3. put the veggies and fry for 5 minutes
